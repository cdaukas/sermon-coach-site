Why these prices?

We've priced this to be sustainable while staying well under the alternatives: a single Simeon Trust workshop runs $400+ once a year, in-person coaching can run $200–500 a month, and most digital tools in the neighborhood sit at $15–50 a month.

And every dollar of proceeds goes to global missions through Global Training Network. I serve as GTN's Executive Vice President; GTN trains indigenous pastors and church planters in the Majority World, where seminary often isn't an option. Common Questions

The details, before you commit.

What counts as one evaluation?
Submitting one sermon as text (manuscript or transcript) and receiving the full dashboard back. You can revise and resubmit the same sermon as a new evaluation if you want to compare drafts.
What happens if I hit my monthly limit?
You can either upgrade to the next tier mid-month (prorated) or wait for your next billing cycle. We don't auto-charge for overages — you stay in control of your spend. If you'd rather not change tiers, you can also buy a pack to bridge the gap.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts. Cancel from your account dashboard at any point — your subscription stays active through the end of the current billing month, then ends. Packs aren't a subscription, so there's nothing to cancel.
What about refunds?
Every subscription comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If The Sermon Coach isn't useful for your preaching after a month of real use, email Chris and you'll get a full refund — no issues. Packs can be refunded within 7 days of purchase if no evaluations have been used.
